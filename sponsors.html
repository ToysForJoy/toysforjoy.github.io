Our Sponsors

Toys For Joy is grateful for the companies and organizations that help make our mission possible. Through donations of materials, equipment, and support, our sponsors help us create and distribute toys to children who need them most.

How Sponsors Help

Our sponsors enable us to expand our reach and impact. Here's how their support transforms our mission:

Filament Donations

Quality 3D printing filament directly supports the production of toys and essentials we donate to children in need.

Equipment Support

Advanced printing equipment and tools enable us to create higher-quality toys more efficiently and reliably.

Production Scaling

With sponsor support, we can increase production capacity and serve more children across hospitals, shelters, and community programs.

Community Impact

Sponsor partnerships amplify our reach, allowing us to bring comfort and joy to more children during their most challenging moments.

Become a Sponsor

Are you interested in partnering with Toys For Joy? We welcome partnerships with companies and organizations that share our mission of bringing joy and comfort to children in need. Sponsorship opportunities are available at multiple levels, and we work with partners to create mutually beneficial arrangements.
