House of Love - Block Party

The House of Love Youth Home's annual block party marked our inaugural event, and we're pleased to report it was a tremendous success. Our team contributed by setting up game booths, facilitating activities, and assisting with cleanup. Participating in such a joyful and meaningful community event provided an excellent foundation for our organization's outreach efforts.


A highlight of the event was sharing our 3D-printed fidget toys with the children. Many had never encountered such toys before, and their genuine excitement and curiosity were deeply rewarding to witness. The children were captivated by the unique designs and eager to take them home. The event organizers also expressed strong appreciation and support for our work, reinforcing the value of our mission.


We extend our sincere gratitude to all donors and supporters whose contributions made this event possible. We look forward to continuing our service and identifying future opportunities to make a positive impact in our community.
