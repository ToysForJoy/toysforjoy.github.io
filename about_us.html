A student-led nonprofit creating and donating handmade toys to bring smiles to children in hospitals and shelters.

Meet Our Team

Rohit Raghunathan

Founder & President

Rohit Raghunathan is a Biomedical Engineering/Pre-medical student at UW-Madison and co-founder of Toys for Joy. Passionate about combining creativity with compassion, Rohit helped launch T4J to design and donate hand-crafted toys to therapy centers and children's shelters. With experience in engineering, research, and community service, he is dedicated to bringing comfort, joy, and hope to children through meaningful, playful experiences. He handles outreach and partnerships to expand Toys for Joy's impact across Wisconsin.

Zakki Mirza

Founder & President

Zakki Mirza is a pre-medical student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison studying Biomedical Engineering. He co-founded Toys for Joy with a mission to bring happiness and comfort to children and families in need through 3D-printed toys and community donations. Combining his passion for healthcare and service, Zakki helps lead initiatives that support children's hospitals, shelters, and local communities across Wisconsin. He is an EMT who uses his skills to help those in need in the community. His goal is to use innovation and compassion to make a lasting difference—one toy at a time.

Anuj Janardhanam

Secretary

Anuj Janardhanam is a sophomore at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, majoring in Biomedical Engineering. Having grown up with hearing loss, Anuj understands the power of medical technology on a deeply personal level. His hearing aids didn't just improve his hearing — they gave him confidence, connection, and a sense of possibility. That experience fuels his drive to help others through engineering and healthcare. He's actively involved in ToysForJoy, a student-led organization that supports children with medical needs. Anuj handles the financial operations — tracking expenses, managing supplies, and building relationships with companies, fundraisers, and local groups to expand the organization's reach. His work reflects a bigger goal: to combine technical skill with empathy and make a meaningful impact in people's lives.

Girish Dodda

Technology Lead

Girish Dodda is a Computer Science master's student at The Ohio State University and the Technology Lead for Toys for Joy. Passionate about front-end development and accessible, responsive design, Girish maintains the website, updates posts and pages, and adds the small details that improve the user experience. With experience in web development, user-centered design, and collaboration, he is dedicated to creating inclusive, safe, and sustainable digital experiences that help bring comfort, play, and hope to children.
