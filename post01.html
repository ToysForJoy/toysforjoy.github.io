Walker's Point Youth & Family Center

We are thrilled to share that we concluded the year with our first delivery of 3D printed fidget toys to the dedicated team at Walker's Point Youth & Family Services. 🎁💖 These toys serve a dual purpose: they bring immediate joy while functioning as therapeutic tools in sessions with young people navigating challenging circumstances. Knowing our creations contribute to helping children feel safer and more comfortable is deeply meaningful to our team.


This milestone reinforced our core mission. Every toy we create, every donation we receive, and every supporter who shares our vision contributes to a larger movement aimed at bringing comfort and joy where it's most needed.


Reflecting on this year, we are grateful for the incredible community support that has enabled us to make a tangible difference in the lives of children and families. Your belief in our mission has been instrumental in our success. As we look ahead to the coming year, we are excited to expand our reach and deepen our impact. Thank you for being part of this meaningful journey. 💫
